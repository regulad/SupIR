<p><i>SupIR</i> is an open-source general-purpose IR blaster for Android using the <a href='https://github.com/probonopd/irdb' target='_blank' rel='nofollow noopener'>irdb</a> database. Inspired by the now-abandoned <a href='https://github.com/OneButtonDeveloper/AndroidInfraRed' target='_blank' rel='nofollow noopener'>AndroidInfraRed</a>, <i>SupIR</i> puts the power of a Universal Remote or a Flipper Zero in your pocket.</p><p><i>SupIR</i> requires an Android phone with an IR blaster. If your phone does not have an IR blaster, unfortunately, you are out of luck.</p>
